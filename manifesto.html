Local-first Manifesto

Your data belongs to you. It should never leave your device without your explicit permission.

Our Principles

1. Data Sovereignty

You own your data. It lives on your device, under your control. No cloud means no third-party access.

2. Privacy by Default

Privacy isn't a feature you opt into - it's the foundation. No tracking, no analytics, no data collection.

3. Offline First

Your tools should work even when you're offline. No internet? No problem. Your AI assistant is always available.

4. Transparency

You should be able to see exactly what's happening with your data. Open source, auditable, verifiable.

5. User Control

Export your data anytime. Delete it completely. You're in charge, not us.

Why Local-first?

The cloud has become the default for everything. But your life data is too personal, too sensitive, too important to trust to someone else's servers.

With modern browsers and WebGPU, we can run powerful AI models entirely on your device. You get the intelligence of AI without sacrificing your privacy.

Join the local-first movement. Take back control of your data. Your life, your device, your AI.
