About OneLife

OneLife is your privacy-first, local AI life assistant. We believe your life data belongs to you, and should never leave your device.

Our Mission

To provide a complete life management solution that runs entirely on your device. No cloud servers, no tracking, no data collection. Just you and your AI assistant, working together locally.

Key Features

  • 100% Local AI - All processing happens on your device
  • Life Records - Automatic data structuring and organization
  • AI Calendar - Natural language event creation
  • Personal Knowledge Base - Local RAG system
  • Life Analytics - Automatic summaries and insights
  • 100+ Tools - Integrated life tools ecosystem

Privacy First

Every piece of data you create in OneLife is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. We use AES encryption for sensitive data, and nothing ever leaves your browser. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.
